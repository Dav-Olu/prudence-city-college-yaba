Curriculum

The School offers comprehensive and qualitative education based on the local 6:3:3:4 educational system, concerning itself with the three junior and three senior secondary sections

The subjects taught on implementation of the full curriculum include:

Arts/Languages Sciences Technical/Vocational Humanity
English Language, French, Literature in English, Yoruba, Social Studies, Christian Religious Knowledge (CRK) and Islamic Religious Knowledge (IRK). Biology, Physics, Chemistry, Economics, Agriculture Science, Basic Science, Mathematics, Information Communication Technology(ICT), Technical Drawing and Further Mathematics. Home Economics, Basic Technology, Business Studies, Fine Arts, Music, Physical & Health Education. Government, History, Insurance, Accounting and Civic Education.
